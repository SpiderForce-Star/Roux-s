Our Story

Family-owned Creole & Cajun cooking made from scratch in downtown Gallatin

Roux’s Creole Cafe brings the soulful flavors of New Orleans and Louisiana to the heart of Gallatin. Owned by Chef James and Holley Lampley, we opened on Mardi Gras 2025 with one mission: authentic Creole and Cajun cuisine made from scratch — no shortcuts.

James is a trained chef with an A.O.S. in Culinary Arts and a B.S. in Hotel/Restaurant Management from the New England Culinary Institute, plus advanced training in Switzerland, Italy, France, Germany, and Monte Carlo. After years at this same location, he and Holley transformed it into a true Creole destination focused on the freshest ingredients and real Louisiana tradition.

We’re a family operation. Our kids help out, Eddie Pollard keeps operations running smoothly, and Sofia Goodall is our beignet queen and bread pudding specialist. Come taste the difference that care makes.
